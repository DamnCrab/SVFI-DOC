    # SR = Super Resolution = Upscale

    Improve the video resolution through some specific algorithms, that is, increase the resolution of each frame of the video to make the look and feel clearer.

    Common algorithms include BICUBICopen in new window, LANCZOSopen in new window, etc., which are not implemented by AI, commonly known as "Resize".

    SVFI uses deep network-based artificial intelligence algorithm RealESRopen in new window, RealCUGANopen in new window for super-resolution, and its super-resolution effect on animation is better than other algorithms.

    Tips

    For the processing task of the same video, SVFI will perform super resolution first, and then perform frame supplementation
